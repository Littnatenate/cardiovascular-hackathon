## Description
**MedSafe Discharge Tool** is an intelligent translation and reconciliation layer that bridges the gap between complex medical records and diverse patient comprehension in Singapore.

### Our Strategy: The "Last-Mile Delivery System"
Instead of replicating hospital EHR databases, MedSafe focuses entirely on translating the final discharge plan into actionable, culturally relevant habits for elderly patients and their caregivers. 

### Why MedSafe Wins (USPs):
1. **Hyper-Localized Translation:** Bypasses generic medical English, adjusting for local health literacy levels and preparing for local dialects.
2. **Caregiver Enablement:** Automatically generates SMS/WhatsApp summaries in languages commonly spoken by Foreign Domestic Workers (Bahasa, Tagalog), bridging the demographic divide between doctors and homecare.
3. **Drip-Feed Education:** Mitigates discharge day info-dumping by sequencing the post-discharge education.
4. **Healthier SG Alignment:** Localizes lifestyle advice (e.g., "Ask for less gravy at the mixed rice stall").

### Pitch Conclusion
*We solve the "I have the pills, but I don't know when to take them or what they do" problem.*
